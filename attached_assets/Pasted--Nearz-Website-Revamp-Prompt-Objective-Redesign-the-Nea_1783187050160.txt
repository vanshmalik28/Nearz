# Nearz Website Revamp Prompt

## Objective

Redesign the **Nearz** website with a premium, modern, and conversion-focused UI/UX while keeping all existing content exactly the same. Do **not** change the website structure or text. The goal is to make the website visually engaging through animations, interactions, and polished design.

---

# Brand Guidelines

### Brand Colors

* Primary: Premium Orange (#FF6A00 or similar)
* Secondary: White (#FFFFFF)
* Accent: Soft gradients using orange shades
* Text: Dark Charcoal (#1A1A1A)
* Background: White with subtle orange glow effects

The overall look should feel:

* Modern
* Premium
* Minimal
* Luxury Salon Marketplace
* Clean
* Fast
* Commercial Quality

---

# Typography

Use **one font family throughout the entire website** for consistency.

Recommended fonts:

* Plus Jakarta Sans (Preferred)
* Alternatively: Manrope or Poppins

Typography should be:

* Bold headings
* Medium-weight body text
* Large spacing
* High readability
* Modern appearance

Do not mix multiple font families.
